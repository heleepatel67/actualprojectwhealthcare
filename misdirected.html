Investments in Healthcare seem Misdirected

  • In many healthcare systems, particularly in privatized models, investments are often driven by profit motives rather than patient needs.

    • Impact:

    This can lead to overuse of unnecessary medical procedures and medications, increased healthcare costs, and potential harm to patients

  • Healthcare investments often prioritize treatment over prevention

    • Impact:

    Preventive measures, such as vaccinations, health education, and lifestyle interventions, can significantly reduce the incidence of chronic diseases and lower overall healthcare costs.
